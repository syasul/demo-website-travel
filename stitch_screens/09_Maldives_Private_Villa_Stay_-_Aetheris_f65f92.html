Maldives Private Villa Stay

location_on Baa Atoll, Maldives

Unparalleled Island Sanctuary

Experience the pinnacle of luxury at our ultra-private overwater villa. Nestled in the heart of the UNESCO World Biosphere Reserve, this retreat offers total seclusion, personalized 24/7 concierge service, and direct access to one of the world's most vibrant coral reefs.

Experience Itinerary

Your journey begins with a private lounge reception at Male Airport followed by a scenic 40-minute seaplane flight over the atolls. Upon arrival at your villa, a chilled bottle of Krug awaits. Spend the evening enjoying a private sunset cocktail session on your deck.

Dive into the turquoise depths with our resident marine biologist. Explore the house reef's hidden caves and meet the local sea turtles. The afternoon is reserved for a restorative deep-tissue spa treatment in the overwater pavilion.

Board our luxury yacht for a short cruise to a deserted sandbank. A five-course lunch prepared by your private chef will be served under a canopy. Return for a moonlight cinema experience on the beach.
